SO... Today, I went to my first pride festival! My goodness!!!

Technically, I'm still partially hiding who I am. I still go by my deadname and dead pronouns, but only because I dont feel comfortable yet asking people to use she/they or call me Amy.

But seeing so many people just be themselves and seeing a FURSUITER for the first time was so so so magical! I have only been able to connect to other trans and queer people online.
Finally seeing the wide range of people in-person was something so amazing. Seeing them, and seeing that they are REAL, was so awesome.
